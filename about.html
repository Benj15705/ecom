About Us

MarketPlus is a non-profit, web-based e-commerce market storefront system tailored for the farmers of the Canitoan Farmers Multipurpose Cooperative of Cagayan de Oro City. The researchers from Liceo de Cagayan University - Senior High School Department created this platform to directly reach out to their customers, minimizing the presence of intermediaries such as middlemen. The website is created to be a medium for direct communication between the farmer and customer, where the prices are decided by the producers themselves.
